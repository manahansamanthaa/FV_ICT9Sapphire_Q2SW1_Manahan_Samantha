Encanto



A Columbian Family, The Madrigals who lived in a magical house called "Casita". They all recieve magical powers.

However, Mirabel, did not. unlike her family.



CHARACTERS

Name Picture Description
Mirabel Protagonist of the story and not gifted. Sister of Isabela and Luisa Madrigal.
Isabela One of Mirabel's older sister. gifted with the ability to grow and make flowers bloom.
Luisa Also one of Mirabel's sister, gifted with super-strength. Able to lift wild animals, bridges, etc.
Abuela Takes charge of the magic in the family. Mother of Julieta, Pepa and Bruno.
Bruno Uncle of Mirabel, Isabela and Luisa. Has the ability to see the future.
Pepa Aunt of Mirabel, Isabela and Luisa. Ability to control the weather.
Dolores Eldest child of Pepa and her husband Felix. Ability to hear from afar.
Camilo Middle child of Pepa and her husband Felix. Ability to shapeshift.
